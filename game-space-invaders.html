Space Invaders

Goal

It is your task to defend planet Earth against the Space Invaders. You can kill the invaders with a laser weapon. Hit the space bar to shoot and the arrow keys to move left or right. You have 3 lives and a wall as defense.


Techniques used

The following techniques were used:

  • Python scripts (with a GUI interface based on the Turtle library)
  • Object-oriented programming (classes "Defense", "Invaders", "Invader", "Lasers", "LaserShooter", "Scoreboard" and "Shot")
